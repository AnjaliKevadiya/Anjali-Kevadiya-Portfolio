Charging Forward

BioDental Sciences is a premier state of the art dental laboratory offering an array of digital dentistry solutions in the heart of New York City. Now there is a simple and convenient way for dental practices to check the status of their Biodental Sciences lab orders and collaborate with Biodental Sciences lab technicians. With the Biodental App, users can track estimated return dates, check order details, upload patient photos, and collaborate with lab technicians in real-time. These features allow doctors to manage patient care in a timely and efficient manner. No phone calls necessary!


Technologies Used
  • Language: Node.js, HTML, CSS
  • Dependencies: Axios, Bcryptjs, Express, Express-session, Js-cookie, Mysql2, Passport, Passport-local, Sequelizer
  • Version Control Software: Git and GitHub
  • Cloud Platform: Heroku

Mindful Meals

Sometimes it can be hard to keep track of your daily intake of nutrients. With the help of our app it will become easier to keep track of your daily intake each day. Just eating healthy sometimes isn't enough, you must eat the correct amount of healthy food. Knowing exactly which nutrients you are putting in your body will help to lead a healthier lifestyle.
