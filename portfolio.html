Biodental

BioDental Sciences is a premier state of the art dental laboratory offering an array of digital dentistry solutions in the heart of New York City. Now there is a simple and convenient way for dental practices to check the status of their Biodental Sciences lab orders and collaborate with Biodental Sciences lab technicians. With the Biodental App, users can track estimated return dates, check order details, upload patient photos, and collaborate with lab technicians in real-time. These features allow doctors to manage patient care in a timely and efficient manner. No phone calls necessary!
